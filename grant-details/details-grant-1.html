Agricultural Grant

Our organisation is a platform that promotes research and technological innovations in the agricultural sector by providing agriculture grants, mainly for capacity development and research in natural resources and related programs to tackle poverty in rural areas. Also, they organize these grants at different levels ranging from the national level to the global level

Although focus areas of this agriculture grant include research in:

  • Agricultural productivity
  • Innovations in agri-food chains
  • Agricultural policies
  • Institutional strengthening
  • Agricultural competitiveness
  • Natural resources

However, there are also little diversions into natural and environmental resources-based projects. Plus funding from this grant is usually within the range of N100,000 to N500,000 for projects of up to four years.

Eligibility

  • Business must be based in Africa
  • Business must be for profit
  • Business must be 0-3 years’ old
  • Applicants must be at least 18 and a legal resident or citizen of an African country.
  • Applicants must be able to communicate effectively – speaking and writing – in English
  • Applicants must be willing to attend all training and mentoring exercises organised by the programme
  • Applicants must not be an employee of the Nigerian Civil Service
