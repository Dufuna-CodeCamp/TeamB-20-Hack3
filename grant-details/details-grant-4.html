Light Manufacturing grant

Light manufacturing is a section of an economy’s secondary industry characterized by less capital-intensive and more labor-intensive operations. Products made by an economy’s light industry tend to be targeted toward end consumers rather than other businesses; it is the production of small consumer goods. The facilities typically have less environmental impact and are usually less capital-intensive than heavy industry. The objective is to provide support for asset and utility acquisition for light manufacturing operations. The fund shall be deployed for the provision of equipment and working capital loans to local companies engaged in light manufacturing across the country. The purpose is to finance the acquisition of production/processing equipment, utilities (generators, transformers, forklifts etc.) and working capital.

TARGET MARKET

Duly registered Limited Liability Companies or Enterprises engaged in light manufacturing activities, which include;

  • Blocks & Interlocking Stones
  • Ceramics & Tiles
  • Chemicals and Paints
  • Cosmetics/Hair Products
  • Plastics
  • Recycling
  • Roofing Sheets
  • Soaps and Detergents
  • Lube Blending
  • Digital Printing/Multimedia Publishing
  • Doors and Window Frames
  • Furniture/Wood Processing

Eligibility

  • Business must be based in Africa
  • Business must be for profit
  • Business must be 0-3 years’ old
  • Applicants must be at least 18 and a legal resident or citizen of an African country.
  • Applicants must be able to communicate effectively – speaking and writing – in English.
  • Applicants must be willing to attend all training and mentoring exercises organised by the programme.
  • Applicants must not be an employee of the Nigerian Civil Service.
