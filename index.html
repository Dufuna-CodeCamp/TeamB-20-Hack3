ABOUT US

Afrigrants is the leading champion of enterpreneurship in Africa. Our objective is to empower women and men across our continent, catalysing growth, driving poverty eradication and ensuring job creation. We belive the private sector's role is critical for Africa's development and that the private sector must create both social and economic wealth.

Process of getting a business grant

  1. Develop an internal strategy for developing the application.
  2. Identify the requirements, outline internal deadlines, engage appropriate stakeholders, and develop the writing approach.
  3. Work towards the submission date.
  4. Review the NOFO (notice of funding opportunity) carefully
  5. When writing, think like a reviewer.
  6. Use language that can be easily understood by peer reviewers, scientists, and the public. Create a well-structured, readable application.
  7. Complete required registrations [Completion of registration can take 3-5 business days.]
  8. Complete and submit the grant application
  9. Save changes to your application as you go. Afrigrants does not automatically save changes. The package cannot be submitted until all required fields are populated. Click the “Submit Application” button on the grant application package cover page.
